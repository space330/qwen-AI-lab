## Style Prompt

Qwen Agent Lab is shown as a premium local-first AI console: dark roasted-brown canvas, amber liquid glass, soft refraction, warm particle depth, and precise technical UI surfaces. The video should feel cinematic, calm, and highly polished: light bends across glass panels, transitions flow like molten amber, and every UI detail supports the idea of a capable local agent.

## Colors

- Background: `#2a170d`
- Deep shadow: `#120905`
- Glass panel: `rgba(255, 220, 165, 0.10)`
- Primary amber: `#d87408`
- Bright amber: `#f2a327`
- Cream highlight: `#ffdca5`
- Readable text: `#fff4df`
- Muted text: `#d9b37a`

## Typography

- Display: `Instrument Serif`, 900 for oversized product statements.
- UI and body: `Space Grotesk`, 300-700 for clean technical copy.
- Data/code: `IBM Plex Mono`, 400-700 with tabular numbers.

## Motion

- Primary transitions: liquid light-leak wipes, blur push slides, and glass refraction zooms.
- Entrances: every scene builds from staggered opacity, scale, mask, and direction changes.
- Ambient: slow particle drift, glass sheen, subtle lens warping, and low-frequency panel breathing.
- Pacing: 7 scenes over 45 seconds; no jump cuts.

## What NOT to Do

- No cyan/purple tech gradients.
- No flat card grid with identical tiles.
- No generic black background.
- No hard cuts between scenes.
- No tiny web-sized typography.
